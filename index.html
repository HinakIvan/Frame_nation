FRAME NATION

Frame Up community in Brno with regular classes, Frame Up workshops in Brno, and masterclasses in Central Europe

FOR DANCERS WHO ARE LOOKING FOR FRAME UP BRNO, A STRONG COMMUNITY, AND REAL GROWTH

  • ✓ Frame Nation is a Frame Up community in Brno built around regular training, support, and artistic growth.
  • ✓ We organize Frame Up workshops in Brno and invite dancers to masterclasses in Central Europe with a strong focus on style, technique, and community. The community is led by Anastasiia Hinak and developed together with teachers who actively grow the Frame Up scene in Brno.
